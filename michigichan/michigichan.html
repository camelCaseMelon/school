Michigan National Parks

Isle Royale Pictured Rocks Sleeping Bear Dunes
Location Lake Superior Lake Superior Shore Lake Michigan Shore
Description Isle Royale is an extremely isolated island in Lake Superior. People go here to kayak, hike, and camp far away from civilization. Pictured Rocks is a national lakeshore on Superior. The cliffs and landscape are breataking year-round, and many unique sites await. Sleeping Bear Dunes is a collection of sand dunes on the shore of Lake Michigan. The dunes provide clear and spectacular views of the lake.
